How to Build!
Want to know how to build your own PC via video or step by step? Find out here!
REMEMBER to do this with supervision if you are young regardless if you are watching the video of doing the step by step process
Step-By-Step process:
    Remember that even
  • Step 1:

    Remove the back screws on the Pc casing and take the side cover off
  • Step 2: Screw the motherboard standoffs into the case If applicable, screw off and take out the rear IO plate and replace it with motherboard IO plate fasten the motherboard in place (make sure it is on top of the mounting standoffs)
  • Step 3: Locate the CPU socket holder, lift up the latch to open the socket cover By lining up the triangle symbols on the CPU chip itself with the symbols on the motherboard, carefully install the CPU
  • Step 4: Install the CPU cooling fan next by seating and fixing the fan in position Remember to plug in the power cable of the cooler fan into the motherboard controller
  • Step 5: To mount the power supply, find the case mounting points and plug in the LARGEST abling connector from the power supply cabling to the motherboard power connector plug the 8-pin cabling connector from the power supply to the CPU power connector
  • Step 6: Now you will mount the memory, find and press the 2 clips at both ends of the RAM mount slots and have the RAM stick line up with it. Press firmly and make sure the tabs automatically latch onto the stick to confirm the installation. Install the rest of your RAM sticks (if needed)
  • Step 7: To install your GPU, first remove the expansion slot covers on the rear side of your case, it will be used to hold your graphics card. Next, install the GPU at the Lower half of the motherboard and line it up properly, make sure to screw in the screws to hold the GPU in place.
  • Step 8: Before mounting your storage devices, make sure that if your storage is a 2.5" size and not a 3"5 size, try to get aan adaptor plate to mount the storage. Mount the storage devices into the drive bays located beside the CPU and motherboard. Fix it with screws from the cases frame into the special case mounting holes. Next, connect the drive via the SATA cable to the motherboard. Followed by the power cabling to the storage drive.
  • Step 9: (OPTIONAL) You will now mount the optical drives. Remove any front panels from the casing first,
  • Step 10: Connecting the front panel connectors and case fans may be complicated (recommend watching the video here), note that if your PC has already installed the case fans, dont need the re-install any Case fans: (optional) Mount your case fans into your case using the screws/clips provided and then connect the case fan power connectors to the multiple fan headers located at different parts of the motherboard. Front panel connectors: Identify the cabling from the front panel ports of your Pc, you will need it to be plugged into the motherboard so that buttons, inputs and outputs on your front panel will work. Connect any audio connectors into the front audio header on the motherboard, followed by any front panel USB's connectors to the motherboard USB headers as well as the motherboard front panel case connectors to the motherboard front panel input or output headers
  • Step 11: Congradulations, you have built your PC! Just place the side cover back on and screw it back into place. Finally, connect outer devices such as mouse, monitors, keyboards etc.
