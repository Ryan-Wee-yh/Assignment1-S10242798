Welcome to Hardware Information!
Find out what the different main components do in PC's and enhance your understanding of PC's!
CPU
The CPU is the brain of a computer, it contains all the circuitry needed to process input, store data, and output results. It manages the processing of data, which is basically how fast your computer will perform.
GPU
The GPU a specialized processor designed to accelerate graphics rendering. making them useful for machine learning, video editing, and mostly on gaming purposes(Good grphics and such). If you want a gaming pc fit to handle the most powerful games at high graphics, then you need a good GPU!
Memory
The memory is the holding place for data a computer needs to reach quickly. It's where information is stored for immediate use. Without it, a computer would not be able to function properly. The bigger your RAM the more tabs you can open on a pc and the faster your speed will be.
Hard Drive
Hard drives store operating systems, software programs and other files using magnetic disks. It is where all your files and downloaded applications/softwaree go to The bigger the hard disk, the more storage you will have.
